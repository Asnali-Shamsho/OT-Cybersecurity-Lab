# Risk Assessment

## Overview
This section focuses on identifying and analyzing potential cyber and operational risks that could affect OT and process automation systems.

## Objectives
- Apply NIST SP 800-30 and ISA/IEC 62443 methodologies for risk assessment.
- Identify threats, vulnerabilities, and potential impacts in OT networks.
- Develop a risk register and define mitigation priorities.

## Tools & Frameworks
- NIST SP 800-30
- ISA/IEC 62443-3-2
- Excel / Word (for documentation)

## Key Tasks
- Created a detailed risk matrix for control and enterprise systems.
- Assessed risk likelihood and impact for each identified threat.
- Documented mitigation strategies and control recommendations.

## Results
A comprehensive OT Cyber Risk Register that supports security planning and prioritization.
